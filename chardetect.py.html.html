20   Script which takes one or more file paths and reports on their detected 
21   encodings 
22    
23   Example:: 
24    
25       % chardetect somefile someotherfile 
26       somefile: windows-1252 with confidence 0.5 
27       someotherfile: ascii with confidence 1.0
